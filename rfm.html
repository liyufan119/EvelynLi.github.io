RFM Modle and Marketing Campaign Analysis

You can find all the code and details of this project on here: RFM Analysis

RFM refers to Recency Frequency Monetary, which is a method used for analyzing customer value. It is commonly used in database marketing and direct marketing and has received particular attention in retail and professional services industries.

RFM stands for the three dimensions:

  • Recency: How recently did the customer purchase?
  • Frequency: How often do they purchase?
  • Monetary Value: How much do they spend?

Customer purchases can be represented by a table with columns for the customer name, date of purchase, and purchase value. One approach to RFM is to assign a score for each dimension on a scale from 1 to 10. The maximum score represents the preferred behavior, and a formula could be used to calculate the three scores for each customer. In this project, my main goal is to create an RFM system for existing customers and analyze the marketing campaign outcome based on different variables to identify patterns that can help improve our marketing strategy.
