Customer Segmentation Analysis

You can find all the code and details of this project on here: Customer Segmentation Analysis

Customer segmentation is the process of dividing a customer base into distinct groups based on specific criteria such as demographics, behavior, interests, or purchasing patterns. This strategy is widely used in retail online businesses to better understand their customers and tailor marketing strategies, product offerings, and customer experiences to meet the unique needs of each segment.

After exploring and cleaning the data, I utilized PCA to reduce the data dimension for modeling and visualization. Next, I employed the Elbow method to determine the optimal cluster number and applied KMeans for clustering. As a result of clustering, the customers have been categorized into 6 distinct groups.
